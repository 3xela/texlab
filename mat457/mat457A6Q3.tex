We have that $$\frac{a}{2}= m(E \cap I) \leq m(\bigcup_{i} F_i ) \leq a ,$$ Since the covering $\{F_i\}$ cover $[0,a]$. Therefore we have that $a=0$ and thus $m(E) = 0$, which contradicts our assumption.
