Q2: Let $z = a + \ci b$. Properties of the modulus tells us that 
\begin{align*}
    \Big| \frac{z-1}{z+1} \Big| & \leq 1 
    \\ \iff \frac{|z-1|}{|z+1|} & \leq 1
    \\ \iff |z-1| & \leq |z+1|
    \\ \iff (a-1)^2 + b^2 & \leq (a+1)^2 + b^2
    \\ \iff a^2 -2a +1 & \leq a^2 + 2a +1
    \\ \iff 0 & \leq 4a
    \\ \iff 0\leq a
\end{align*}
Hence the region defined by the inequality will be $R = \{a+\ci b \in \C : a\geq 0 \}$.
