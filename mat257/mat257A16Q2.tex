We see that $g(1) = \int_{[0,1]}fdt  - \int_{[0,1]} fdx=0$ and $g(0)= \int_{[0,0]}fdt - 0=0$. The uniqueness of $\lambda$ follows from the uniqueness of $\int_{[0,1]} f dx$.
